What is One Click Post?

One Click Post is the simple Utility Android Application that allows you to post Text, Pictures, Videos and Links to Facebook Groups (NOT on your Facebook Wall or Pages) which you have subscribed. You can post using your own Facebook account only.

You need to compose your post once only and the post will be automaically posted to selected Facebook Groups.
