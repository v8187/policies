Privacy Policy

Last updated on February 01, 2020

What is One Click Post?

One Click Post is the simple Utility Android Application that allows you to post Text, Pictures, Videos and Links to Facebook Groups (NOT on your Facebook Wall or Pages) you have subscribed to. You can post using your own Facebook account only.

You have to compose your post once only and then select Facebook Groups, where you want to post. This application will post to all groups automatically.

How does One Click Post collect data about me?

One Click Post does not collect any data at any moment in any format through any medium.

The information/data Application needs to work properly, will be stored on your own device only, on which the application is installed.

One Click Post does not buy or otherwise receive data about you from data brokers.

What data does One Click Post collect about me, and why?

One Click Post does not collect any data at any moment in any format through any medium.

The information/data Application needs to work properly, will be stored on your own device only, on which the application is installed.

One Click Post collects payment card data.

To buy licenses and sign up for One Click Post Pro, One Click Post requires your payment card data. One Click Post does not collect or store enough information to charge your card itself. Rather, One Click Post's payment processors collect that data on One Click Post's behalf, and gives One Click Post security tokens that allow One Click Post to create charges and subscriptions.

One Click Post uses your payment card data only to charge for One Click Post products and services. One Click Post instructs its payment processors to store your payment card data only as long as you use paid One Click Post services.

How can I make choices about data collection?

You're free to visit oneclickpost.com without logging in. You may also configure your web browser to disable cookies when visiting the website.

You can opt out of Google Analytics using a browser extension.

One Click Post does not respond to the Do Not Track HTTP header.

Where does One Click Post store data about me?

One Click Post stores account data, data about website use, and data about content delivery network use on servers in the United States of America.

Content delivery network servers are located worldwide, and store data about their use wherever they are located.

Does One Click Post comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation?

One Click Post respects privacy rights under Regulation (EU) 2016/679, the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Information that GDPR requires One Click Post to give can be found throughout these questions and answers.

Where can I see, change, and erase data about me?

You can see and change your account data at any time by visiting your account page on oneclickpost.com.

You can cancel your One Click Post Pro subscription at any time. Canceling subscription will also start the process of deleting your account data.

Does One Click Post share data about me with others?

One Click Post does not sell or give information about you to other companies or services. However, One Click Post does use services from other companies to provide oneclickpost.com, content delivery networks, and to distribute and develop and software. The companies behind those services may collect data about you on their own, for their own purposes. Some of these services may be used to collect information about your online activities across different websites. All of these services are based in the United States.

How can I contact One Click Post about privacy?

You can send privacy-related questions, complaints, and requests to One Click Post at privacy@oneclickpost.com.

For complaints under GDPR more generally, European Union users may lodge complaints with their local data protection supervisory authorities.
