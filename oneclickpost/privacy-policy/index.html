Privacy Policy

Last updated on February 01, 2020

What is One Click Post?

One Click Post is the company home of One Click Post, libraries of free-to-use and paid . As a company, One Click Post publishes on its website, oneclickpost.com, as well through content delivery networks for use on customers' websites.

One Click Post sets only its own privacy policy, not the privacy policies of One Click Post customers or others who use One Click Post. You should ask all of those involved in administering and hosting websites that you use for their privacy policies.

How does One Click Post collect data about me?

One Click Post collects data about you:

  • when you browse oneclickpost.com
  • when you use One Click Post's content delivery networks, or visit websites that do
  • when you create and use an account on oneclickpost.com
  • when you buy products and services from One Click Post through the oneclickpost.com web store
  • when you contact One Click Post by e-mail for support or other help

One Click Post does not buy or otherwise receive data about you from data brokers.

What data does One Click Post collect about me, and why?

One Click Post collects data about visits to oneclickpost.com.

When you visit oneclickpost.com, whether you have an account or not, the site uses cookies, server logs, and other methods to collect data about what pages you visit and when.

One Click Post uses data about how you use the website to:

  • optimize the website, so it's quick and easy to use
  • diagnose and debug technical errors
  • defend the website from abuse and technical attacks
  • compile statistics on popularity
  • compile statistics on the kinds of software and computers visitors use

One Click Post usually stores data about how you use the forum in identifiable form for just a few weeks. In special circumstances, like extended investigations about technical attacks, One Click Post may preserve log data longer, for analysis. One Click Post stores aggregate statistics about use of the website indefinitely, but those statistics don't include data identifiable to you personally.

One Click Post collects data about use of its content delivery networks.

Content delivery networks are worldwide networks of computer servers that make sure everyone online can download files quickly from servers near to them. One Click Post uses content delivery networks to serve files for its free and Pro. Many websites using One Click Post have visitors download the from One Click Post's content delivery networks.

When you visit a website that uses a One Click Post content delivery network to load, One Click Post collects data about what files you download and when.

One Click Post uses data about how you use One Click Post content delivery networks to:

  • optimize the content delivery networks, so website visitors can download files quickly
  • diagnose and debug technical errors
  • defend the content delivery networks from abuse and technical attacks
  • calculate fees for One Click Post Pro customers
  • compile statistics on popularity
  • compile statistics on the kinds of software and computers visitors use

One Click Post usually stores data about how you use content delivery networks in identifiable form for just a few weeks. In special circumstances, like extended investigations about technical attacks, One Click Post may preserve log data longer, for analysis. One Click Post stores aggregate statistics about use of content delivery networks indefinitely, but those statistics don't include data identifiable to you personally.

One Click Post collects account data.

To buy One Click Post Pro, access Pro files, and configure Pro services like content delivery networks, you need a oneclickpost.com account. To sign up for an account, One Click Post requires your name, your company's name, if any, and your e-mail address.

One Click Post uses your account data to identify you on oneclickpost.com.

One Click Post uses your e-mail address to:

  • announce new versions of Pro, software, and services
  • advertise new One Click Post products and services
  • send you alerts about Pro services
  • reset your password and help keep your account secure
  • contact you in special circumstances related to your account
  • contact you about legal requests

One Click Post stores your account data as long as your account remains open.

One Click Post collects payment card data.

To buy licenses and sign up for One Click Post Pro, One Click Post requires your payment card data. One Click Post does not collect or store enough information to charge your card itself. Rather, One Click Post's payment processors collect that data on One Click Post's behalf, and gives One Click Post security tokens that allow One Click Post to create charges and subscriptions.

One Click Post uses your payment card data only to charge for One Click Post products and services. One Click Post instructs its payment processors to store your payment card data only as long as you use paid One Click Post services.

How can I make choices about data collection?

You're free to visit oneclickpost.com without logging in. You may also configure your web browser to disable cookies when visiting the website.

You can opt out of Google Analytics using a browser extension.

One Click Post does not respond to the Do Not Track HTTP header.

Where does One Click Post store data about me?

One Click Post stores account data, data about website use, and data about content delivery network use on servers in the United States of America.

Content delivery network servers are located worldwide, and store data about their use wherever they are located.

Does One Click Post comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation?

One Click Post respects privacy rights under Regulation (EU) 2016/679, the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Information that GDPR requires One Click Post to give can be found throughout these questions and answers.

Where can I see, change, and erase data about me?

You can see and change your account data at any time by visiting your account page on oneclickpost.com.

You can cancel your One Click Post Pro subscription at any time. Canceling subscription will also start the process of deleting your account data.

Does One Click Post share data about me with others?

One Click Post does not sell or give information about you to other companies or services. However, One Click Post does use services from other companies to provide oneclickpost.com, content delivery networks, and to distribute and develop and software. The companies behind those services may collect data about you on their own, for their own purposes. Some of these services may be used to collect information about your online activities across different websites. All of these services are based in the United States.

How can I contact One Click Post about privacy?

You can send privacy-related questions, complaints, and requests to One Click Post at privacy@oneclickpost.com.

For complaints under GDPR more generally, European Union users may lodge complaints with their local data protection supervisory authorities.
